UKRI Strategy 2022 - 2027

The 5 year strategy sets a five-year vision for UKRI to catalyze transformational change in the UK’s R&I system, ensuring it remains world-class and delivers benefits for society, the economy, and the environment. By fostering collaboration, innovation, and sustainability, UKRI aims to make the UK a global science superpower and innovation nation.

Objectives

Our strategy is structured around six objectives. These objectives will ensure the UK has the people, institutions, infrastructures and partnerships to be a global science superpower with the world’s most innovative economy, attracting globally mobile business and talent

1. World class People

We want to attract, develop and retain global talent, so we can strengthen the UK's research and innovation syste. Want to diversify career paths and skills, and create flexible opportunities across displines and sectors. We aim to create a supportive culture that encourages creativity and values collaboration, empowering people to drive innovation and address global challenges effectively.

2. World class Places

We are strengthening R&I clusters and partnerships, improving financial sustainability, and investing in cutting-edge infrastructure to drive innovation and maintain the UK’s global leadership in research and innovation.

3. World class ideas

We're promoting diverse, high-quality research portfolios, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, and supporting emerging trends and breakthrough innovations to advance knowledge and drive transformative solutions.

4. World-class innovation

We are boosting private sector investment in R&D, accelerating the translation of research into commercial applications, and enhancing knowledge exchange to drive innovation-led economic growth and global competitiveness.

5. World-class impacts

We're addressing national and global challenges, leveraging transformative technologies, and driving innovation in key sectors to position the UK at the forefront of solutions for a sustainable, healthy, and secure future.

6. A world class Organisation

We are improving efficiency, agility, and collaboration within UKRI, reducing bureaucracy, and championing environmental sustainability and ethical practices to drive impactful and responsible research and innovation.

UKRI is a partnership of the UK's seven research
councils, Innovate UK and Research England.
