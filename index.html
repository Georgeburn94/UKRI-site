Welcome to WeAreUKRI

Advancing research and innovation across the UK

About us

WeAreUKRI is a partnership of the UK's seven research councils, Innovate UK and Research England. We work with universities, research organisations, businesses, charities, and government to create the best possible environment for research and innovation to flourish. We aim to maximise the contribution of each of our component parts, working individually and collectively. We work with our many partners to benefit everyone through knowledge, talent and ideas.

Our Work

We fund and support research and innovation in the UK, working with universities, research organisations, businesses, charities, and government.
